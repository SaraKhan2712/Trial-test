Some Interesting Facts About Platy Fish

  1. Often red; other types include black, tuxedo and sunset platyfish
  2. About 1.5 to 2.5 inches long
  3. Often bump against the glass with their lips when hungry
  4. Like to nibble on algae
  5. Not very demanding
  6. Hardy
  7. Peaceful, but not timid; do not bother any other fish in the tank
